The WebApp is a tool designed for mental health professionals that facilitates appointment management, data recording, and tracking of patients' clinical history. With an intuitive system, t allows organizing all relevant information in one place, enhancing efficiency and ensuring personalized and secure care.

About Us

PsyMed is a startup formed by a group of university students committed to creating an app that facilitates mental health management. Our mission is to support doctors in efficiently managing appointments, recording data, and tracking patients' clinical histories, all on an intuitive and secure platform. We aim to improve the quality of care and optimize the work of mental health professionals.
